About

This is simply the tech news site I always wished I had, collecting prominent tech news sites and coding blogs in one gory place. Enjoy at your own peril.

It uses a frecency algorithm to ensure that spammy feeds don't overwhelm the stream too much, keeping more rare content in the loop as well.

Contact

Contact me at daniel@@d2ab.org or ping me on Twitter @@d2aborg for comments, feedback, suggestions etc, or if you wish to have your blog removed from this aggregator.
